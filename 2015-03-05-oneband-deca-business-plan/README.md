- `king-county-household-income-histogram.png`: King County household income distribution histogram.
- `united-states-household-income-histogram.png`: United States household income distribution histogram for comparison.
- `microsoft-band-exploded-hardware-view.png`: Exploded hardware view diagram of the Microsoft Band.
